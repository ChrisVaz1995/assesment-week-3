How does the internet work? What happens when you click on a link? 

The internet is a large infrastructure that is connected to the Web. 
It allows the computer to have access to web pages and communicate with others. 

When clicking on a link, it is doing many things in the background in matter of seconds. 
It then follows a procedure of steps to get the website that you're looking for.
